Frequently Asked Questions

1. Account and Login

I forgot my password, how can I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email address and a password reset link will be sent to you.
I cannot log in with Google or Apple?
Please check your device's internet connection. For Google Sign-In, make sure Google Play Services is up to date. iOS 13+ is required for Apple Sign-In.
How can I delete my account?
You can permanently delete your account from Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This action cannot be undone.

2. Premium Features

How do I start my Premium membership?
Click Profile > Upgrade to Premium. You can start with a 7-day free trial. If you don't cancel, you'll be automatically charged after the trial ends.
How do I cancel my subscription?
iPhone: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Sugar Guide > Cancel Subscription

The subscription remains active until the end of the period.
What are the Premium features?
Access to trigger mapping, relapse management, cognitive exercises, 600+ alternatives discovery, time capsule, and new pleasures features.
I canceled the free trial but was charged?
You need to cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends. If you think you were charged by mistake, contact us at support@sekerrehberim.com.

3. Data and Synchronization

My data is lost, how can I recover it?
Make sure you're logged in with the same account. Data is stored on cloud servers. If you have an internet connection, tap Profile > Synchronization.
Is my data saved in offline mode?
Yes, all data is saved locally on your device and automatically synchronized when you have an internet connection.
I changed my device, how do I transfer my data?
Log in with the same account credentials on your new device. Your data will be automatically loaded.

4. App Usage

How do I record daily progress?
On the home screen, click either "No Sugar" or "Had Sugar" button. You can optionally add notes.
Barcode scanner not working?
Make sure you've granted camera permission for Sugar Guide in Settings > Privacy > Camera.
Not receiving notifications?
Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings > Notifications > Sugar Guide. Check your preferences in-app from Profile > Notification Settings.

5. Technical Issues

App won't open / crashes?
Try the following steps:
  • Force close the app and reopen
  • Restart your device
  • Check for updates in the App Store
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your data will remain safe in your account)
What iOS version is required?
iOS 13.0 or higher is required.
App is running slow?
Make sure you have enough storage space on your device. Try closing other apps running in the background.

6. Privacy and Security

Is my data safe?
Yes, all data is protected with AES-256 encryption and stored on secure servers. GDPR and CCPA compliant.
Who can see my data?
Only you. Your data is not shared for advertising or other purposes.
Can I get a copy of my personal data?
Yes, you can request a data copy by emailing support@sekerrehberim.com.

7. Payment and Billing

What payment methods do you accept?
All payment methods accepted through Apple App Store (credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, gift card).
Can I get an invoice?
Your App Store invoice from Apple will be emailed to you.
Can I get a refund?
Refund requests are subject to Apple App Store policies. You can request at reportaproblem.apple.com.

8. Other Questions

Can I use the app offline?
Yes, all basic features are available without internet. Educational content requires internet for initial download.
How do I change the language?
You can select Turkish or English from Profile > Settings > Language.
Is the app free?
Basic features are free. Premium features require a subscription (7-day free trial).
